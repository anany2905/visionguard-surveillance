About VisionGuard

Revolutionary surveillance technology powered by artificial intelligence

Our Mission

VisionGuard is an advanced surveillance system designed to revolutionize workplace safety and security. Using cutting-edge Computer Vision technology, we provide intelligent monitoring solutions that help organizations protect their assets, ensure employee safety, and optimize operations.

Our system combines three powerful use cases into one unified platform, enabling real-time monitoring and automated responses to security and safety concerns.

Our Advanced Use Cases

Face Detection & Authorization

Our advanced facial recognition system verifies personnel identity in real-time. When a person enters the monitored area, the system instantly identifies them and displays:

  • Person Name: Full identification of authorized personnel
  • Personnel ID: Unique employee identifier
  • Authorization Status: Immediate confirmation of access rights
  • Bounding Box: Visual tracking with secure data overlay

The system maintains accuracy of 99.8% and updates every frame for real-time verification.

PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) Detection

Workplace safety is paramount. Our PPE detection system monitors and verifies that all personnel are wearing required equipment:

  • Helmet Detection: Confirms head protection is worn
  • Safety Vest Detection: Ensures visibility clothing is in place
  • Gloves Detection: Verifies hand protection
  • Safety Boots Detection: Confirms foot protection is equipped

Real-time alerts notify supervisors of non-compliance, preventing workplace accidents before they happen.

Box Counting

Count the number of boxes in images and live video feeds for real-time inventory management:

  • Image Analysis: Upload images to automatically count total boxes
  • Live Video Stream: Real-time box detection and counting from video feeds
  • Instant Results: Get accurate box counts in seconds
  • Inventory Tracking: Monitor box quantities for warehouse operations
  • User-Friendly: Simple interface for quick analysis

Streamline inventory management, reduce manual counting, and improve warehouse efficiency with automated box detection.
